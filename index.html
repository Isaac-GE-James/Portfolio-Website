About Me

I am a game developer located in south of London, looking to get into the games industry. I've been making games and art throughout my life, completing game james as well as my own personal projects. Programming has been a hobby of mine since I first learnt about it, starting with python in 2017, then moving on to unity and c# in 2018. In recent years I've moved form unity to the open source game engine Godot, form most of my projects, however I still enjoy using unity form time to time. Another hobby of mine has been 3D modeling, where I began to use Blender in late 2019. Since I was very young I would make stop motion animations with things like lego and clay, so moving to 3D modeling was a natural next step for me. I'm eager to work in the games industry to meet people with the same passion as me, as well as to learn more about the craft I've dedicated myself to.
